Chronix was born from an idea that seemed impossible in late 2015, in Da Nang city where the dynamism of a coastal metropolis meets the innovative spirit of a startup city. Eight founding members, with diverse backgrounds in Quantum Physics, Nanotechnology, Artificial Intelligence, Industrial Design, and Business, came together to pursue a shared dream: to challenge the ultimate definition of time. They asked: "If we can measure time with such precision, why can't we interact with it?" After 11 years of secret research and development in a privately funded laboratory, Chronix officially launched in January 2026, becoming the first company in the world to announce and successfully commercialize a line of wristwatches capable of physical interaction with the flow of time at the user level: The Infimity Watch.
